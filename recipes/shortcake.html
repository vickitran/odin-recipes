ChatGPT's Vegan Strawberry Shortcake

The vegan shortcakes are tender and fluffy, with a hint of sweetness that perfectly balances out the tartness of the fresh strawberries. The strawberries themselves are perfectly ripe and bursting with flavor, and the addition of a sprinkle of sugar before layering them onto the shortcakes really brings out their natural sweetness. The vegan whipped cream is a lovely finishing touch, adding a creamy richness that ties everything together. Overall, this is a vegan strawberry shortcake that truly stands out and is not to be missed.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened plant-based milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • Additional sugar, for sprinkling on strawberries
  • Vegan whipped cream, for serving (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    3. Cut in the cold vegan butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse sand.
    4. Add the plant-based milk and vanilla extract, and stir until the dough comes together.
    5. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead it a few times until it is smooth.
    6. Pat the dough out into a circle that is about 1 inch thick. Use a biscuit cutter or a glass to cut out rounds of dough. You should get about 6 shortcakes.
    7. Place the shortcakes on a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until they are lightly golden.
    8. Remove the shortcakes from the oven and let them cool slightly.
    9. While the shortcakes are cooling, toss the sliced strawberries with a sprinkle of sugar.
    10. To assemble the shortcakes, split each shortcake in half horizontally. Place the bottom half on a plate, top with a spoonful of sugared strawberries and a dollop of vegan whipped cream, if using. Add the top half of the shortcake, and repeat with the remaining shortcakes.
    11. Serve the shortcakes immediately, or store them in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. Enjoy!
